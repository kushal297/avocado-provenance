Status

A proof point can be valid or invalid. To be considered valid a proof point must pass several authenticity and integrity checks backed by cryptography and blockchain. Due to the properties of cryptographic signatures and the distributed consensus algorithm of the Ethereum blockchain, it is technologically infeasible to forge a valid proof point.
